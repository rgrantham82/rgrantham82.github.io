Statement

Icons don’t beg the rule of thirds; they claim the center. The lotus stands like an altar figure while Sursum Corda (lift your hearts) arcs a benediction and the cropped Blackletter Resi– hints at Resilientia. Blue and gold—ancient as a cathedral—stay lively in thin metallic layers that catch gallery light without glare.

Process & Materials

  • Warm umber imprimatura and block‑in establish silhouette and ground (see WIP below).
  • Petal depth: transparent ultramarine+umber glazes, finished with restrained titanium highlights.
  • Thin metallic gold field for a responsive, light‑catching surface.
  • Lettering integrated into the composition: Copperplate (liturgy) + Blackletter (structure).
