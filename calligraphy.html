About Me

I am Robert Grantham, a calligrapher and artist with a passion for blending modern calligraphy with street art influences. I use cardboard not only because it's easily accessible, but also to keep it out of landfills, promoting sustainability. My work is a tribute to friendship, urban life, and eco-consciousness.
