Studio Capabilities

More than paintings on walls.

Sometimes a space demands more than a framed object. It requires an intervention. These services cover murals, site-specific lettering, and practical consulting to ensure the work commands the room exactly as intended.

Murals & Scale

Architectural interventions

For businesses, community spaces, offices, and ambitious interiors. I don't paint cartoon mascots. I deliver sweeping, calligraphic, and abstract statements that redefine the architecture of a room. This includes comprehensive planning, digital mockups, premium materials, and professional on-site execution.

Best for: Feature walls & brand-forward interiors

Custom Calligraphy

The written word as structure

Hand-lettered pieces for gifts, memorials, events, and installations. Rendered on museum-quality paper, panel, or mixed-media formats. When a text holds weight—a vow, a poem, a founding document—it deserves to be rendered with the gravity of history, not spit out by a laser printer.

Best for: Meaningful text & permanent records

Commission Consulting

Strategic placement & curation

Buying art should not feel like guessing the weight of a carnival pig. If you are an interior designer or a new collector unsure of what scale, palette, or placement will actually serve your space, I provide direct consulting to ensure the final piece anchors the room rather than getting lost in it.

Best for: New collectors & design professionals

Workshops & Demos

Passing the fire along

Small-group sessions on the mechanics of contemporary calligraphy, structural composition, and the unforgiving reality of layering acrylics. We strip away the mystical nonsense and focus on the practical discipline of making a mark that matters.

Best for: Creative teams & community programs

Ready to scope a project?

The process begins with a conversation. Let me know what you're building, the rough dimensions, and the timeline, and I will give you a straight, no-nonsense answer on what it takes to execute it.
