How Quotes Work

  1. Scope: what, where, dimensions, surface, deadline.
  2. Mockup: concept sketch or digital comp when needed.
  3. Estimate: materials + labor + travel + finish.
  4. Schedule: deposit reserves dates; remainder due on completion.

If your budget is tight, tell me early. I can often adjust scope, materials, or scale. What I won’t do is pretend it’s cheap and then ambush you later.

Best info to send

  • Location + indoor/outdoor
  • Wall dimensions + photos
  • Desired style (link examples if you have them)
  • Deadline window
  • Budget range
