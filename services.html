Services

More than paintings on walls

Sometimes you don’t need a single object; you need a space to speak. These services cover murals, lettering, and practical help getting art and signage to look intentional instead of improvised.

Murals & large-scale lettering

For businesses, community spaces, offices, and interiors. Includes planning, mockups, materials, and on-site execution.

Best for: feature walls, entry statements, brand-forward interiors.

Custom calligraphy

Hand-lettered pieces for gifts, memorials, events, and installations. Paper, panel, or mixed-media formats.

Best for: meaningful text that deserves more than a printer.

Commission consulting

If you’re unsure what to commission (size, palette, placement), I can help you decide so the final work fits naturally.

Best for: new collectors and first-time commissioners.

Workshops / demos

Small-group sessions on calligraphy basics, composition, and layering techniques with acrylic and matte mediums.

Best for: community programs, student groups, creative teams.
