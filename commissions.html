How to order (short)

  1. Choose a package from the Commission Menu below (Pocket Praise, Cornerstone, Statement, Bespoke).
  2. Contact: DM on Instagram @InkedOnCardboard, email {{ site.email | default: 'robertgrantham40@gmail.com' }}, or call/text (512) 200-3563.
  3. Pay the indicated deposit (if required) → approve mockup → I work → pickup or shipping arranged.

Turnaround & delivery

  • Small (Pocket Praise): 3–7 days (full payment up front).
  • Medium (Cornerstone): 7–14 days (50% deposit required).
  • Large (Statement): 2–4 weeks (50% deposit required).
  • Bespoke & Series: 3–8+ weeks depending on scope.

Policies

Payment & refunds

  • Payment methods: Cash, PayPal, Venmo, Cash App. Invoices by arrangement for commercial work.
  • Deposits: 50% deposit required for Medium+ projects; Small pieces paid in full up front. Deposits reserve time and materials and are non-refundable.
  • Cancellations: If you cancel prior to work start, deposit is retained. If I cancel (rare), full deposit refunded.

Revisions

  • Medium+ projects include up to 2 mockup revisions. Extra revisions are billed at $25 each.
  • I send a single photo proof after completion; minor touch-ups are included. Major rework is treated as a revision and charged accordingly.

Copyright & reproduction

  • Buyers own the physical original. Digital images for personal or promotional use are included.
  • Commercial reproduction / exclusive licensing is negotiated separately (quote based on scope and print run).

Shipping & pickup

  • Local pickup in Austin preferred — I can deliver to pickup partners by arrangement (e.g., Medici Coffee).
  • Shipping is charged at cost + $10 handling. International shipping quoted separately.

Materials & care

  • Most works are on reclaimed cardboard or gessoed panels and sealed with matte varnish. They are not museum-grade unless specially treated.
  • Framing is recommended for long-term preservation; framing service available as an add-on.

FAQ

Can you letter long quotes or prose?
Yes — longer text usually requires larger size or multi-panel work. Ask for a Bespoke quote with the full text and preferred size.
Do you accept returns?
No returns on commissioned originals. If the final piece materially differs from the approved mockup, I will offer a remedy (repair, partial refund, or replacement where feasible).
Do you accept trades or barter?
Occasionally, for local businesses or community projects. Standard pricing applies; barter considered in addition to partial payment for supplies or studio needs.
Can I request color matching or special materials?
Yes — specialty inks, gold Posca, and other materials may add to cost. These are included in the quote.
How many revisions are included?
Two mockup revisions included for Medium+ projects. Additional revisions: $25 each.
