The *Threshold* series considers the painted surface as a place of passage—where color, rhythm, and structure meet at the edge of transformation. In *Threshold of Night*, that passage is quieter, deeper, and more atmospheric.

Here, blue becomes more than color; it becomes space, pressure, and mood. I wanted the painting to feel nocturnal without becoming still, layered without becoming heavy, and abstract while still carrying the sense of an interior world just beyond description.
