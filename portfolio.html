My Portfolio

Welcome to My Calligraphy Gallery

I’m Robert, a street artist based in Austin, Texas, channeling resilience and personal narrative through expressive calligraphy on unconventional canvases—mostly cardboard. Each piece is a moment, a message, and a mirror reflecting both tradition and rebellion.

AI as a Creative Companion

At Cardboard Calligraphy by Robert, I use AI primarily as a source of inspiration and assistance throughout the creative process. From conceptualizing initial designs to refining intricate details, AI helps me explore new artistic possibilities while maintaining the authenticity of my handmade work. Every piece is carefully crafted and uniquely mine, with AI augmenting—never replacing—the human touch.
