About

Robert Grantham is an abstract calligrapher based in Austin, Texas. Trained in vocal performance and holding a history degree, his work merges disciplined structure with expressive gesture.

His practice explores rhythm, sacred text fragments, movement, and material presence through canvas and street-calligraphy panels.
