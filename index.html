About Me

Hi, I'm Robert Grantham, a dedicated and passionate data analyst with a strong background in AI, predictive analytics, and data visualization. With a Bachelor's degree in History from the University of Southern Mississippi and over six years of experience in loss mitigation and content review, I have honed my analytical skills and developed a keen eye for detail.

My journey into data analytics began in 2020, and since then, I have worked on various projects involving SQL, machine learning, and data visualization. My goal is to leverage data to drive meaningful insights and help organizations make informed decisions.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a 1st degree black belt in Aikido, which has taught me discipline, focus, and the importance of continuous improvement.

Currently residing in Austin, TX, I am actively seeking full-time employment as a data analyst. I am committed to making a positive impact through data and look forward to contributing my expertise to a forward-thinking organization.
