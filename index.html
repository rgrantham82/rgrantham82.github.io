Robert Grantham Art · Austin, Texas

Contemporary paintings built from color, pressure, memory, and place.

I make original acrylic paintings that explore resilience, symbolic form, and the emotional architecture of color. The work is direct, layered, and made to hold a room without merely behaving in it.
